Whether you're arriving early or staying a few days after the conference, Boise offers plenty of winter-friendly activities, food, drinks, and local culture.

## Arts, Culture & History

### [Boise Art Museum](https://www.boiseartmuseum.org/)
Contemporary art and sculpture garden in Julia Davis Park.  
Location: 670 Julia Davis Dr  
Hours: 10 AM – 5 PM  
Admission: $6

### [Idaho State Museum](https://history.idaho.gov/museum/)
Interactive exhibits on Idaho’s environment, Indigenous heritage, and state history.  
Location: 610 Julia Davis Dr  
Hours: 10 AM – 5 PM  
Admission: $10

### [The Basque Block](https://www.boisebasqueblock.com/)
Explore Boise’s Basque heritage with food, wine, museums, and music.  
Don’t miss [Bar Gernika](https://www.bargernika.com/) for croquetas and cider!
